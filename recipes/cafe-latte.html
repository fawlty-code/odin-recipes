Cafe Latte Recipe

Cafe latte is a traditional Italian coffee drink which comes out perfectly creamy and topped with frothy foam. Get ready to make a barista-quality cafe latte in the comfort of your own home.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hot freshly brewed dark roast espresso coffee
  • 2 cups milk
  • Sugar Cubes/Sweetener (Optional)

Directions

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan set over medium-low heat.
  2. Whisk briskly with a wire whisk to create foam.
  3. Brew espresso and pour into four cups.
  4. Pour in milk, holding back the foam with a spoon. Spoon foam over the top.
